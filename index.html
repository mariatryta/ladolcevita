Our history

Our restaurant opened in 2014 with the purpose to share our love for food with our guests. We truly believe that eating is one of the best things that can happen to us, especially if we can eat exclusive meals.

In La Dolce Vita we make your dishes according to this principle. I mean, look at the name. It means “the sweet life” and we take that really serious in our restaurant and try to make your moments in our restaurant unforgettable!

Restaurant

Experience a piece of Northern Italy in an old timbered house. Italian cusine is considered one of the best and our chef truly mastered the art of cooking. We bring the taste of Italy and the passion of its nation into Helsingbor to provide you with delicous experiences while you enjoy the traditional yet sophisticated interior of our restaurant.

We focus on fresh and quality-made ingredients. Our homemade pasta is the reason why guest visit us again and agian.
